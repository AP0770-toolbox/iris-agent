Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

1. Purpose

Iris Agent is a private TikTok creator analytics assistant. It helps an authorized creator review their own TikTok account analytics, content performance, and audience insights for planning and reporting.

2. Information Processed

When authorized, Iris Agent may process TikTok account and creator analytics data such as profile information, video performance metrics, audience insights, and related reporting fields made available through TikTok's developer tools.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide private analytics summaries, performance review, trend analysis, and planning assistance for the authorized creator. Iris Agent does not use TikTok data to automate posting, commenting, messaging, or engagement.

4. Sharing

Iris Agent is intended for private use by the authorized creator or application operator. TikTok data is not sold. Data is not shared with third parties except as needed to operate the service, comply with law, or with the authorized user's consent.

5. Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to provide the analytics assistant functionality, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, or support debugging and audit needs. Data may be deleted when no longer needed.

6. Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect data processed by Iris Agent. No system can guarantee absolute security, but access should be limited to authorized operators and authorized creator accounts.

7. User Control

Authorized users may revoke application access through TikTok account settings where applicable. For deletion or privacy requests, contact the repository owner or application operator.

8. Contact

For privacy questions about Iris Agent, contact the repository owner or application operator.
